What do we do?

Our job is simple.. you focus on the grades, we focus on getting you paid!

Step 1: Join Our Mailing List

By joining our mailing list, you sign up to automatically receive weekly newsletters with job postings right near YOUR college campus! Every week check your email to see who's hiring, and when something sticks out at you, send us an email and we'll be in touch!

Step 2: We Connect You To The Employeer

Avoid all the stress of aimlessly throwing your resume at employers. We do all the heavy lifting for you! Once you reply to one of our emails, or send us your resume down below, we will link you straight to the employer.

Step 3: Get To Work

Our job is now over and we are turning things over to you! But don't worry, you'll get compensated for your work 😋. Now that we got you in touch, enjoy your new position as a service worker where you'll make lifelong friends, as well as some hefty tips!

Meet The Team

The Experts

Josh Hageman

CEO, Co-Founder

Josh Hageman is a recent West Chester graduate whose passions lie in finance. He has a degree in finances with years of managerial experience. Managing several restaurants during covid led him to notice a problem in the recruiting portion of the business. There was a high demand in employees, and not enough people applying for the jobs.

Josh also noticed that the difficulty of paying off student loans was only increasing. He wanted to solve both problems at once by creating a service that both employed students to help them pay off loans, as well as provide restaurants with hard working employees.

Rayli Raykov

CTO, Co-Founder

Rayli Raykov is a veteran developer who is ServingU's head of technology. Having worked in the industry several years, Rayli takes care of everything ranging from gathering email data, aligning students with clients that meet their wants and needs, and making sure the transition into the work world is as seamless as possible. Since a teenager, Rayli has worked as a service worker or in the bar and restaurant scene.

What once started out as a bus boy job at Macaroni Grill over a decade ago, lead to a bartender position and post-graduation into a software role. Having worked in the restaurant business has given Rayli a chance to meet some of his closest friends and the fortune of being able to graduate with $0 dollars’ worth of debt. If you need anything, do not hesitate to contact him in his social media provided below!
